Echo Bot — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. What we collect

Server IDs, channel IDs, user IDs, and message content necessary for bot functionality. Message content is processed ephemerally and not stored permanently.

2. How we use it

To respond to commands, maintain conversation context, and provide memory features. No data is sold or shared.

3. Data retention

Conversation context is retained per-session and may be persisted in memory files. You may request deletion at any time.
